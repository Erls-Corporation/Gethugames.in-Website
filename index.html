Tic Tac Toe Extended

This is an extended version of Tic Tac Toe game with a board size of 9x9. The player scores a point on placing his sign in 4 consecutive tiles. The game can be played with another human player or with AI. At the end of the game based on player's score and timing a Badge is awarded which can be shared to Facebook / Twitter.
